Great that you made it to my blog. This blog is a hobby project of myself and a place to play around with various technologies. About the style: I really like to iterate when I work. That's also how I will proceed with this blog. Start with the most minimalistic thing that improves something. Focus on it and get it live quickly. After that tackle the next problem. All of the articles will have a very clear goal I try to achieve and the article will explain what I did to achieve that goal and also some of the thoughts why I decided to go for a certain solution. Maybe there will also be an outlook, if I do already have some follow up topics in mind. So let's go and see where thejourney will lead us.
